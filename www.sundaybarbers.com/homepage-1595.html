Frequently Asked Question

What services are offered at Sunday Barbers?

Sunday Barbers offers a range of services, including haircuts, styling, beard trims, and grooming treatments. Our skilled team of barbers is experienced in providing personalized services tailored to your unique style and preferences.

Are walk-ins accepted at Sunday Barbers?

While we primarily operate by appointment, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to secure your preferred time slot and ensure prompt service.

What are the operating hours of Sunday Barbers?

Sunday Barbers operates from Tuesday to Sunday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Mondays.

How can I schedule an appointment at Sunday Barbers?

Booking an appointment is quick and easy! Simply click the "Schedule Now" button on our website, choose your preferred date and time, and follow the prompts to complete your booking.

How long does a typical haircut session at Sunday Barbers take?

The duration of a haircut session may vary depending on the complexity of the style and individual preferences. On average, a haircut session at Sunday Barbers takes approximately 30 to 45 minutes.
